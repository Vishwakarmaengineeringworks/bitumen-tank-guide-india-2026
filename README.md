# Bitumen Tank Selection Guide for Contractors (India 2026)

## Overview

This repository contains the **Bitumen Tank Selection Guide for Contractors: Road Construction Equipment Planning in India (2026)** published by Vishwakarma Engineering Works.

The guide helps contractors, infrastructure developers, builders, and road construction professionals understand the importance of proper bitumen storage, equipment planning, and efficient road construction workflows.

## What You'll Learn

* How to choose the right Bitumen Tank
* Importance of bitumen storage in asphalt production
* Factors affecting tank performance and efficiency
* Role of Wet Mix Plants in road base preparation
* Asphalt Hot Mix Plant planning considerations
* Road Roller selection insights
* Equipment planning best practices for infrastructure projects

## Key Topics Covered

* Bitumen Tank Selection
* Bitumen Storage Management
* Road Construction Equipment Planning
* Wet Mix Plant Operations
* Asphalt Hot Mix Plant Workflow
* Road Roller Applications
* Infrastructure Project Efficiency
